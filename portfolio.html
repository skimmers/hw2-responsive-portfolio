Trailblazers

The goal of our site is to help ambitious hikers be more informed about the top trails in America.Once a user signs in, they will have access to a list of the top trails in America. By navigating to a trail's page, the user will be presented with relevant information about the trail. For example, the difficulty of the trail, if pets are allowed, and more. They will see comments about the trail from reviewers and have the ability to post comments as well.
